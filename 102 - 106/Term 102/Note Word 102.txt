استفاده برای زمان حال :

I + am > I am / I'm :

You + Are > You are / You're :
We + Are > We are / We're :
They + Are > They are / They're :

He + Is > He is / He's :
She + Is > She is / She's :
It + Is > It is / It's : 

منفی کردن صفات :
اضافه کردن not به آخرش

I am + not > I'm not  :

You Are + not > You're not (or) You + aren't :
We Are + not > We're not (or) We+ aren't :
They Are + not > They're not (or) They + aren't :

He Is + not > He's not (or) He + isn't :
She Is + not > She's not (or) she + isn't:
It Is + not > It's not  (or) it + isn't :

نکته : عموما از این استفاده میشود یا شبیه این

pizza isn't good

For example : 
He's happy != He's not Happy

I'm programmer != I'm not programmer

You're student != You're not student
