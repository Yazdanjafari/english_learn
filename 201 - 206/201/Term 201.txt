چه زمانی از whose استفاده میکنیم ؟
زمانی که میخواهیم سوالی راجب چیز های ملکی بپرسیم
for example : 
whose book is this ? (این کتال برای کیست ؟)

= (normally possessive) = (end of sentences possessive)
Grammer | person = normally possessive = end of sentences possessive :

me = my = mine
he = his = his 
she = her = hers
it = it's = it's 
you = your = yours 
we = our = ours
they = their = theirs

for example :
this is my book 
this book is mine 
this book belong to me 

hi has a dog 
his dog is beautiful

they habe Tshert
their tshert is long
